## 1. Definitions

**"Points"** — Units of contribution tracked in the shared Contribution Ledger.

**"Contribution Ledger"** — A shared spreadsheet (currently Google Sheets) accessible to all Participants, showing all point allocations, balances, and calculations.

**"Surplus Profit"** — Net profit remaining after:
- All operating expenses
- Tax obligations
- Reinvestment Reserve (defined in Section 4)

**"Profit Pool"** — 40% of Surplus Profit, distributed quarterly to Participants.

**"Payout Ratio"** — Participant's Points ÷ Total Points of all active Participants.

---

## 2. Nature of Relationship

This Agreement creates a **contractual profit-sharing arrangement**, not:
- Employment
- Equity ownership
- Partnership
- Agency

Participant has **no voting rights** in the Company. The Founder retains 100% of voting shares.

| Contribution Type | Multiplier | Example |

---

## 4. Reinvestment Reserve

Surplus Profit is calculated **after** deducting the Reinvestment Reserve:

**Formula:** 20% of trailing 12-month revenue OR €50,000, whichever is **lower**.

This ensures:
- Company maintains operating runway
- Reserve scales with business size
- Clear, auditable calculation

---

## 5. Profit Distribution

### 5.1 Timing

Distributions occur **quarterly** (March, June, September, December) within 30 days of quarter end.

### 5.2 Minimum Balance

Payouts require minimum accumulated balance of **€1,000**.

Balances below €1,000 roll over to the next quarter.

### 5.3 Calculation

```
Participant Payout = Profit Pool × (Participant Points ÷ Total Points)
```

### 5.4 Fees

Payment processing fees (bank transfers, currency conversion) are deducted from Participant's payout.

---

## 6. Transparency Requirements

The Company commits to providing all Participants:

1. **Quarterly P&L statement** within 15 days of quarter end
2. **Access to Contribution Ledger** (view and comment)
3. **Annual summary** of all distributions made
4. **30 days notice** before any changes to this Agreement

---

## 7. Trial Period

**First 3 months** from signing:
- Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice
- Accumulated points paid out at **50% value**
- No minimum balance requirement during trial exit

After 3 months, full terms apply.

---

## 8. Inactivity and Forfeiture

### 8.1 Inactivity Definition

No logged contributions for **12 consecutive months**.

### 8.2 Pause Mechanism

Participant may request a **Contribution Pause** (max 6 months):
- Written notice required before inactivity begins
- Points frozen, not forfeited
- One pause per 24-month period

### 8.3 Forfeiture

After 12 months inactivity without approved pause:
- Points forfeit to the general pool
- Participant may re-join under new agreement

---

## 9. Exit and Buyout

### 9.1 Voluntary Exit

Participant may exit at any time with 30 days notice.

Outstanding balance paid:
- At next scheduled quarterly distribution, OR
- Company may offer immediate buyout at **90% of balance value**

### 9.2 Company Buyout Right

Company may buy out any Participant's balance at **90% of value** with 60 days notice.

3. **Minimum 30 days notice** before closing to allow Participant review
